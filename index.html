How Superdesign works

  1. Describe the screen

    Point at an existing component, paste a screenshot, or describe the page in plain English. Superdesign reads your context first.

  2. Generate a draft

    Get a faithful first-pass design — built with accessibility, semantic structure, and your existing stack in mind.

  3. Iterate with variants

    Branch the draft into dark mode, minimal, marketing, or your own prompt. Compare side-by-side, keep what works.

  4. Extend to full flows

    One approved page becomes a consistent multi-page flow. Hand-off ready code stays close to your repo conventions.
